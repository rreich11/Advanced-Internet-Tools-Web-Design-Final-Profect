Work Out Routines

"A Workout for All Levels"

Intermediate
  • 45 seconds: Runners Lunge Stretch (left)
    1. Begin in a plank position with hands directly below shoulders
    2. Step your left foot forward to the outer edge of you mat next to your left pinky finger
    3. Relax through your hips and back, letting them sink toward the ground
  • 45 seconds: Runners Lunge Stretch (right)
    1. Begin in a plank position with hands directly below shoulders
    2. Step your right foot forward to the outer edge of you mat next to your right pinky finger
    3. Relax through your hips and back, letting them sink toward the ground
  • 45 seconds: Pulsing Lunges (left)
    1. Stand under the bar with your right foot about 2 feet in front of your left
    2. Slowly sink down into a lunge, keeping your front knee behind your toes
    3. The press up almost to the starting position. Slowly sink again, and pop up to the three-quarters position
    4. pulse for at least 10 reps on one side.
  • 45 seconds: Pulsing Lunges (right)
    1. Stand under the bar with your left foot about 2 feet in front of your right
    2. Slowly sink down into a lunge, keeping your front knee behind your toes
    3. The press up almost to the starting position. Slowly sink again, and pop up to the three-quarters position
    4. pulse for at least 10 reps on one side.
  • 45 seconds: Push-Ups
    1. Place hands on the floor directly underneath your shoulder
    2. Position your toes on the ground
    3. Look foward and lower your chest until it's a few inches from the ground
    4. Keep your body straight from heel to head
    5. Exhale and push back up quickly
  • 45 seconds: Straight Leg kickbacks (Left)
    1. Start on all fours with your knees under your hips and your hands under your shoulders
    2. Extend you left leg straight out to the back and start lifting it up until you feel the squeeze in back
    3. Lower the leg back down to the floor
  • 45 seconds: Straight Leg kickbacks (Right)
    1. Start on all fours with your knees under your hips and your hands under your shoulders
    2. Extend you right leg straight out to the back and start lifting it up until you feel the squeeze in back
    3. Lower the leg back down to the floor
  • 45 seconds:Rainbow Leg Lifts (left)
    1. Get down on your hands and feet
    2. Raise backside up so that your legs are straight with feet on the floor
    3. Raise your left leg up and out to the side of your body so that your toe touches the floor
    4. Raise your leg up as high as possible while forming an arc and bring it over your other leg and touch the ground with your toes
  • 45 seconds:Rainbow Leg Lifts (right)
    1. Get down on your hands and feet
    2. Raise backside up so that your legs are straight with feet on the floor
    3. Raise your right leg up and out to the side of your body so that your toe touches the floor
    4. Raise your leg up as high as possible while forming an arc and bring it over your other leg and touch the ground with your toes
