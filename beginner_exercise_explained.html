Work Out Routines

"A Workout for All Levels"

Beginner
  • 30 seconds: Step Jacks
    1. Stand with your feet together and your hands at your sides
    2. Take a lateral step and clap your hands above your head at the same time
    3. Return to the starting position
    4. Repeat
  • 60 seconds: Half Wall Sit
    1. Position your back against the wall and slowly squat and stop when your legs are at a 45 degree angle
    2. Back against the wall and weight towards the heels of the feet
    3. Keep this position for 60 seconds
    4. Knees do not extend over toes
  • 45 seconds: Knee Push Ups
    1. Balance on your hands and kneew, your body forming a straigh line from your knees to your head
    2. Bend your elbows and lower your body to the floor, stopping just before your ches touches the ground
    3. Press yourself back to start
  • 45 seconds: Standing Bicycle Crunch
    1. Stand with your legs at slightly wider than shoulder width
    2. Put your hands behind your ears
    3. Lower your left elblow and raise your right knee, crunching them together on a diagonal line. Don't try to jump or hop, but engage your abs for a better workout
    4. Return to the starting position
  • 45 seconds: Standing Hip Circles
    1. Begin standing on one leg, leaning on a wall for support if needed
    2. Raise the unsupported knee to 90 degrees. This will be your starting position
    3. Open the hip as far as possible, attempting to make a big circle with your knee
    4. Perform this movement slowly for a number of repetitions , and repeat on the other side
  • 60 seconds: Full Plank
    1. Get into plank position by supporting your body weight on your hands and toes
    2. Hands should be placed directly under your shoulders
    3. Keep your abdominals contracted and your back straight, eyes ahead of you
  • 45 seconds: Side leg Raises (Right)
    1. Stand with your feet together and your arms on your hips
    2. Take your right leg out, hold it for a moment and then bring it back down
    3. Take your leg high as you can comfortably but don't push too hard
  • 45 seconds: Side leg Raises (Left)
    1. Stand with your feet together and your arms on your hips
    2. Take your Left leg out, hold it for a moment and then bring it back down
    3. Take your leg high as you can comfortably but don't push too hard
  • 2 minutes: Walking High Knees
    1. Stand tall with arms at your sid and feet shoulder-width apart
    2. Walk in place and raise your left knee up towards your chest as high as you can and swing your arms
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (Left)
    1. Lay on your stomach and bend your elbows 90 degrees and rest your weigh on your forearm
    2. Rotate 90 degrees so all weight is on one forarm
    3. Hold position
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (Right)
    1. Lay on your stomach and bend your elbows 90 degrees and rest your weight on your forearm
    2. Rotate 90 degrees so all weight is on one forearm
    3. Hold position
