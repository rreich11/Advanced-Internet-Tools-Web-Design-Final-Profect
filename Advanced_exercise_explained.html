Work Out Routines

"A Workout for All Levels"

Advanced
  • 60 seconds: Jumping Jacks
    1. Stand with your feet together and your hands at your sides
    2. Jump and kick your legs out to shoulder width and bring your hands over your head
    3. Jump and get back to starting position
  • 45 seconds: Abdominal Crunches
    1. Lie face up on the floor with knees bent
    2. With your hands behind ears, raise your body off the floor
    3. Hold for 1 second and slowly return to starting positon
    4. repeat
  • 60 seconds: Step-Ups Onto Chair
    1. Stand in front of the chair
    2. Step onto the chair with one foot, followed by the other
    3. Pause and step down
    4. Then step up the chair aagin leading with the other foot
  • 60 seconds: Squats
    1. Stand with your feet wider than your hips and feet pointed slightly out
    2. Begin bending your knees until parallel to the floor with your back as straight as possible
    3. Push back up until standing position
    4. Repeat
  • 60 seconds: Tricep Dips
    1. Sit on floor with knees bent, feet flat
    2. Lean torso back 45 degrees and place palms on floor under shoulders, fingers facing out
    3. Bring left knee to chest and place left foot atop right knee
    4. Bend elbows directly behind you and lift hips a few inches
    5. Repeat
  • 60 seconds: High Knees Running in Place
    1. Run in place while lifting your knees as high as you can
    2. Swing your arms as if running normally
  • 45 seconds: Lunges (left)
    1. Keep your upper body straight, with your shoulders back and relaxed and chin up
    2. Step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at a 90 degree angle
    3. Push against your heels to return back to starting position and repeat with other leg
  • 45 seconds: Lunges (right)
    1. Keep your upper body straight, with your shoulders back and relaxed and chin up
    2. Step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at a 90 degree angle
    3. Push against your heels to return back to starting position and repeat with other leg
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (left)
    1. Lay on your stomach and bend your elbows 90 degrees and rest your weight on your forearm
    2. Rotate 90 degrees so all weight is on one forearm
    3. Hold position
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (right)
    1. Lay on your stomach and bend your elbows 90 degrees and rest your weight on your forearm
    2. Rotate 90 degrees so all weight is on one forearm
    3. Hold position
