Kale Pesto with Brown Rice Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz. brown rice pasta
  • 1 cup basil leaves (loosely-packed)
  • 1 cup kale leaves (loosely-packed, stems removed)
  • 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese (plus more for garnish)
  • 1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp. lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/3 cup olive oil

Instructions:

Boil a medium sized pot of water, and add the brown rice to the pot. In a separate pot boil the basil, kale leaves, and garlic together. Add the Parmesan cheese to the mixture of leaves and mix until fully melted. Add the rest of the ingredients to this mixture and mix them all together. Poor the mixture of ingredients over the pasta, and stir together. Put the pasta in the oven on 350 for fifteen minutes. Serve immediately.
