Work Out Routines

"A Workout for All Levels"

It's important to keep track of your exercise routine. Each day before you exercise select the date, and record it somewhere for yourself. It's useful to look back on...

Date:

Beginner

Days a week: 2-3

  • 30 seconds: Step Jacks
  • 60 seconds: Half Wall Sit
  • 45 seconds: Knee Push Ups
  • 45 seconds: Standing Bicycle Crunch
  • 45 seconds: Standing Hip Circles
  • 60 seconds: Full Plank
  • 45 seconds: Side leg Raises (Right)
  • 45 seconds: Side leg Raises (Left)
  • 2 minutes: Walking High Knees
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (Left)
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (Right)

Click here for step-by-step instructions of each.

intermediate

Days a week: 3-4

Complete exercise regiment from 'Beginner' and the additional added exercises below:

  • 45 seconds: Runners Lunge Stretch (left)
  • 45 seconds: Runners Lunge Stretch (right)
  • 45 seconds: Pulsing Lunges (left)
  • 45 seconds: Pulsing Lunges (right)
  • 45 seconds: Push-Ups
  • 45 seconds: Straight Leg kickbacks (Left)
  • 45 seconds: Straight Leg kickbacks (Right)
  • 45 seconds:Rainbow Leg Lifts (left)
  • 45 seconds:Rainbow Leg Lifts (right)

Click here for step-by-step instructions of each.

Advanced

Days a week: 4-5

Complete exercise regiments from 'Beginner' and 'Intermediate' in addition to the added exercises below:

  • 60 seconds: Jumping Jacks
  • 45 seconds: Abdominal Crunches
  • 60 seconds: Squats
  • 60 seconds: Tricep Dips
  • 60 seconds: High Knees Running in Place
  • 45 seconds: Lunges (left)
  • 45 seconds: Lunges (right)
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (left)
  • 45 seconds: Side Plank (right)
