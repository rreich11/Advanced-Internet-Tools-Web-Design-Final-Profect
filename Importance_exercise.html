Top Reasons Why Exercise is So Important!

  • Exercise makes a person feel happier. It has been proven that exercising regularly can improve your mood. It produces changes in the parts of the brain that regulate stress and anxiety. It can also increase brain sensitivity to the hormones serotonin and norepinephrine, which relieve feelings of depression.
  • Exercise helps a person lose weight. Exercising increases the bodies metabolic rate, which burns calories and helps a person to lose weight.
  • Exercise is good for your muscles and bones. When a person exercises, it plays an important part in keeping the skeletal system healthy. Also, it helps to build bone density when a person is in their youth and it helps to prevent the disease of Osteoperosis later on.
  • Exercising gives a person more energy. When a person moves a lot, their hearty begins pumping more blood. This in turn sends more oxygen to the bodies muscles.
  • Exercising is beneficial to one's skin! Believe it or not exercising can help maintain one's skin and slow it down from aging.
  • Exercise promotes one's memory and is good for the general health of the brain. When the heart rate increases, the heart pumps more blood which then sends more oxygen to the rest of the body, including the brain.
  • Exercise helps for a good night's sleep. When a person works out, their body heat temperature rises from the exercise. This helps the quality of sleep as it helps to regulate the body temperature drops during sleep.
