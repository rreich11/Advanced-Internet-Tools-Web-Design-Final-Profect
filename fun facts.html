Food Fun Facts:

  1. Dark Chocolate
    • The first chocolate bar was created in 1910.
    • Dark chocolate has many health benefits. It can improve bad cholesterol, lower blood pressure, reduce stress, and improve vision.
    • A Harvard study found that drinking two cups of hot chocolate a day can prevent memory decline.
  2. Nectarines
    • The word nectarine means 'Sweat like nectar.'
    • Nectarines originated in China about 2,000 years ago, and were introduced in Europe around the 17th century.
    • Nectarines are a high source of vitamins C and A, they are abundant in antioxidants, they aid in weight loss, regulate blood pressure levels, and improve immunity and heart health.
  3. Raspberries
    • Raspberries are a member of the rose family.
    • An unripe raspberry that was picked does not ripen.
  4. Broccoli
    • Broccoli contains more protein than steak.
  5. Apples
    • Apples give you more energy than coffee.
  6. Pistachios
    • Pistachios are the seeds of a fruit.
    • Omega-3 fatty acids present in pistachios can help lower blood pressure and may also reduce the risk of heart disease.
  7. Caesar Salad
    • Caesar Salad originates from the Mexican city of Tijuana.
    • Caesar Salad dressing is supposed to look like a vinaigrette.
  8. Avocados
    • Avocados are a fruit. They are classified as berries.
    • Avocados must be picked when they are mature in order to ripen.
    • In Spain and Mexico, avocados are called “alligator pears” due to their shape and bumpy, green skin, while in India and China, they are referred to as “butter pears”.
    • Avocados are very good for your health. They contain vitamin C, E, K, and B-6.
  9. Hot Dogs
    • Americans consume about 20 million hot dogs a year.
    • About 18.5 million hot dogs were consumed during the 2015 Major League Baseball season.
  10. Water
    • Water is very good for your health. Enough water can help you lose weight, reduce heart disease and cancer, flush toxins out of your body, reduce cavities and tooth decay, and help you stay focused.
  11. Peanut Butter
    • Peanut butter is very good for your health. It can help lower cholesterol, aid in weight loss and prevent type 2 diabetes
    • Peanut butter glows in the dark after it is exposed to intense light.
  12. Bananas
    • Bananas can float in water.
    • Bananas can help improve your mood and lower blood pressure.
    • Rubbing the inside of a banana peel on a mosquito bite can help keep it from itching.
    • Humans share about 50% of their DNA with bananas!
